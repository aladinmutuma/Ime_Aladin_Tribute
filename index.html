Nelson Mandela

Nelson Mandela during his heydays

  • 1913: Mandela was born in Umtata, South Africa
  • 1933: Began his Secondary education at Clarkeberry Methodist High School in Engcobo.
  • 1937: Methodist college
  • 1939: Fort Harare University. Got BA degree
  • 1941: Started working.
  • 1943: started Law degree.
  • 1944: Married Everlyn Mase
  • 1950: Joined ANC's National executive
  • 1952: Arrested for fighting against Apartheid
  • 1956: Received a ban on public appearances.
  • 1961: Founded Umkhonto we Sizwe
  • 1962: Arrested again
  • 1964: Sent to Robben Islands for 18years
  • 1982: transferred to Poolsmoor Prison
  • 1990: Released from Prison
  • 1991: Elected ANC president.
  • 1994: Elected as president of South Africa.
  • 1999: Retired from politics.
  • 2013: Died.
